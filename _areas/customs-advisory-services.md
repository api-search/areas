description: Customs advisory services help importers, exporters, and trade professionals navigate complex customs regulations, tariff classifications, valuation rulings, country-of-origin determinations, free trade agreement qualification, and trade compliance obligations. Advisory services span licensed customs brokers, trade attorneys, the Big Four professional services firms, specialized trade consultancies, and the rulings and guidance issued by national customs authorities such as U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP), the World Customs Organization (WCO), and the European Commission's Taxation and Customs Union. APIs in this space are generally embedded in larger trade compliance platforms, global trade management (GTM) suites, and customs broker software rather than offered as standalone products.
